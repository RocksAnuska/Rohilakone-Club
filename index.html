About Us

Rohilakone Amra Kojon Club is a vibrant community dedicated to celebrating Indian culture, sports, and festivals. Our club brings together people of all ages to participate in cultural programs, sports tournaments, and traditional festivities. We believe in unity, joy, and the spirit of togetherness, making every event memorable for our members and guests.
